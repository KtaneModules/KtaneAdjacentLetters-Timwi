On the Subject of Adjacent Letters

UP, down, left, right, UP, down, left, right...!

  • Every button will have a different letter on it.
  • Check the table to determine which buttons to press.
  • Each letter in the “letter” column that has one of the letters in the “next to” column next to it on the keypad must be pressed.
  • Each letter in the “letter” column that has one of the letters in the “above/below” column above or below it on the keypad must be pressed.
  • Press Submit to submit your answer. Note: Multiple buttons can be pressed and they can be in any order.
